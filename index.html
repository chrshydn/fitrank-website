Frequently Asked Questions

What is FitRank?

FitRank is a social fitness app that lets you create and join challenges with friends. Track exercises like push-ups, pull-ups, squats, and more. Compete on leaderboards, earn streaks, and stay motivated by turning your workouts into friendly competition.

How do challenges work?

Create a challenge by picking an exercise, setting a goal, and inviting friends with a share code. There are two types: Group Goal challenges where everyone works together toward a collective target, and Free For All challenges where you compete head-to-head for the top spot on the leaderboard.

Is FitRank free to use?

Yes! FitRank is completely free to download and use. All features — unlimited challenges, inviting friends, tracking workouts, and climbing leaderboards — are free. We also offer optional Supporter plans for anyone who wants to help keep FitRank growing, but they're not required to use any part of the app.

What exercises can I track?

FitRank supports a variety of exercises including push-ups, pull-ups, squats, sit-ups, lunges, burpees, and more. Each challenge focuses on a single exercise, making it easy to track progress and compare with friends.

Is FitRank available on Android?

Yes! FitRank is available on both iOS and Android. Download it from the App Store or Google Play.

What are Supporter plans?

Supporter plans are optional, pay-what-you-want contributions that help keep FitRank free for everyone. There are three tiers — Supporter, Champion, and Legend — starting at $9.99/year. They're a way to support the app's development and help the community grow. FitRank is and will always be fully free to use — Supporter plans are just for those who want to give back.
